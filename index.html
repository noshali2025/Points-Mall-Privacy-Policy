1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

1.1. Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request support, or otherwise communicate with us[cite: 3]. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Information: First name and last name, Email address, and Phone number[cite: 3].

  • Authentication Data: Passwords (encrypted) or OAuth tokens if you choose to sign in via Google Sign-In[cite: 3].

1.2. Usage Data and Activity

We collect data regarding your activity within the application to manage your points balance and rewards[cite: 3]. This includes:

  • Points earned through daily check-ins, spinning the wheel, and viewing advertisements[cite: 3].
  • Order history, including items purchased, points spent, and delivery status[cite: 3].
  • Referral codes generated by you and the status of users who join using your code[cite: 3].

When you access the Service by or through a mobile device, we may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device unique ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browser you use, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Points Mall uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including the management of your wallet balance and points[cite: 3].
  • To fulfill and deliver physical rewards to your specified address in Pakistan[cite: 3].
  • To notify you about changes to our Service, order statuses (e.g., "Out for delivery"), and promotional offers[cite: 3].
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so (e.g., the Weekly Leaderboard)[cite: 3].

  • To monitor the usage of our Service and detect, prevent, and address technical issues.
  • To serve rewarded advertisements through our advertising partners to allow you to earn points[cite: 3].

3.1. Firebase & Google Cloud

We use Firebase (provided by Google) for backend infrastructure, authentication, database storage, and file hosting. The following Firebase services are utilized:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used to securely manage user sign-ups, logins, and email verification[cite: 3].
  • Cloud Firestore: Used to store user profiles, points balances, order history, and app settings securely in the cloud[cite: 3].
  • Firebase Storage: Used to store application assets and product imagery[cite: 3].

For more information on what data Firebase collects, please visit the Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

4.1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. We provide an in-app mechanism for you to do this seamlessly:

  1. Navigate to the Profile tab within the Points Mall app[cite: 3].
  2. Select Settings[cite: 3].
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section and tap Delete Account[cite: 3].
  4. Confirm your choice. Your account and all associated data will be permanently marked for deletion and processed within 24 hours[cite: 3]. This action cannot be undone.
